FitHub Design

  • Category: Figma Design

This is my FitHub design, showing simple exercise options.

The purpose of the exercise project was to design a new feature for an existing exercise app, focusing on a "Planks Challenge" feature. The goal was to provide users with a predefined challenge centered around the plank exercise, offering detailed information and customizable options. Throughout the project, I utilized skills in user interface design, information architecture, and user experience principles. Technologies such as graphic design software and prototyping tools were employed to create mockups and layouts. One problem encountered during the project was determining the best way to incorporate user input for the workout duration while maintaining simplicity and intuitiveness. To solve this, I implemented customizable options for the plank challenge duration, allowing users to input their preferred duration directly or choose from preset options. This approach balanced flexibility with ease of use, enabling users to personalize their workout experience effectively. In terms of design decisions, I opted for a clean and minimalist layout to enhance usability and focus on key elements such as exercise instructions and timer display. A simple color scheme with contrasting colors was chosen to improve readability and draw attention to important information. Functionality-wise, options for pausing, resuming, or skipping exercises were included to provide users with control and flexibility during the challenge. Overall, the design prioritized clarity, functionality, and user customization to deliver an engaging and effective workout experience.
